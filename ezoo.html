ABOUT

EZoo is an interactive and educational mobile app designed to bring the wonders of a zoo to your fingertips. Perfect for animal enthusiasts, students, and families, EZoo provides a rich and immersive experience that allows users to explore, learn, and engage with a virtual zoo from anywhere in the world.

Challenge

Goal

My objective is to provide an engaging and user-friendly experience for a virtual zoo experience using Ezoo.
